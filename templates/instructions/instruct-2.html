Instructions

Note that this experiment is a timed experiment.

Once you begin the video portion of the experiment, a timer will start and will appear at the top right corner of the screen for the duration of the experiment. You will be able to complete the experiment within a few minutes but you will have a total of 15 minutes before the experiment expires. Once an experiment expires, the study will exit and your data will not be recorded. This means that the experiment will be unable to be completed. The reason this can happen is to ensure that you have completed the experiment to the best of your abilities and ensure that we have the most accurate data available. You do not need to worry about the time limit as you will have ample time to finish the experiment, the timer is only for your own knowledge.
