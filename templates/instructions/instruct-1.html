Instructions

Thank you for participating in the following robotics experiment, a study of which focuses on human-robotic interaction (HRI).

You will read a few brief pages of instructions before filling out some information about yourself. None of this data can be used to identify you and is only collected as a means to understand how different people may interpret the same situation uniquely.

You will be asked basic information such as your gender, your age, experience, and your Prolific ID. Please have this information ready.
